About La Maison Intégrée

A structured home for SMEs — built on clarity, transparency, and growth.

Our Story

La Maison Intégrée began with one mission: to give SMEs the structure they need to grow.

Many small and medium enterprises work tirelessly, yet their numbers fail to reflect that effort. Receipts get lost, stock gets manipulated, and expenses vanish without trace — leaving owners unable to trust their financial records.

We built La Maison Intégrée as a solution: a refined “House of Clarity” where SMEs regain control through structure, visibility, and strong internal systems.

Today, we support businesses across multiple sectors — offering bilingual communication (EN/FR), internal control frameworks, professional organization, and digital tools like Trackify, designed specifically for African and ECOWAS markets.
